News

Pitt Meadows Plumbing & Mechanical Systems (2001) Ltd. and its predecessor companies have been involved in every aspect of Mechanical Contracting and have also provided general contracting services to project owners. Although centred in the Lower Mainland, our target market has grown to include multiple regions of British Columbia including Vancouver Island and the Interior. Our extensive experience, knowledgeable project managers, committed long-term employees and progressive approach to innovation and technology provide us with a distinct advantage over our competition when it comes to design-build ventures.
